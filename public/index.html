About Me

My IT career, beyond basic usage, began with me going to college at Indiana State University, where I earned my Bachelor's in C.S. After college I joined the navy as a network technician working on a system called "Ship's Self Defense System" or SSDS. SSDS is basically a network the interconnects all the weapon's systems, radars, sensors, and HF communications into a cohesive interface to allow designated Navy servicemen to be able to protect the ship's personnel and infrastructure. Unfortunately, as you progress in rank, the more you are pulled away from the technical aspect of the job and into pure office administration. After talking to my family we decided it was time to move on. From there I went to Microsoft's Software and Systems Academy which, ironically, brought me to the AWS apprenticeship partner Code Fellows. I hope to use the skills I will gain with CF to work on backend cryptography and digital key signing.
